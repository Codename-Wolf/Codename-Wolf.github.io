Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the login page and click on the "Forgot Password" link. Follow the instructions to reset your password via email.

2. What is the refund policy?

Our refund policy allows you to request a refund within 14 days of purchase as long as the game has less than 2 hours of playtime. Contact support for assistance.

3. How can I contact customer service?

You can contact our customer service team by filling out the form above, or emailing us directly at support@gamestore.com.
